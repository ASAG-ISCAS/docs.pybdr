# Van der Pol oscillator

## Equations

$$
\frac{d^2X}{dt^2}-\mu (1-X^2) \frac{dX}{dt} +X =0
$$

We provide a two-dimensional form as 

$$
\dot{x}=y \\\\
\dot{y}=\mu (1-x^2)y-x \\\\
\mu =1
$$
