Van der Pol oscillator​

d2Xdt2−μ(1−X2)dXdt+X=0\frac{d^2X}{dt^2}-\mu (1-X^2) \frac{dX}{dt} +X =0dt2d2X​−μ(1−X2)dtdX​+X=0

We provide a two-dimensional form as

x˙=yy˙=μ(1−x2)y−xμ=1\begin{align*} \dot{x} &=y \\ \dot{y} &=\mu (1-x^2)y-x \\ \mu &=1 \end{align*}x˙y˙​μ​=y=μ(1−x2)y−x=1​
